In the Beginning...

Ted and Betty Russon have been happily married for 30 years. They fondly recall their adventurous honeymoon at Arches National Monument. Their shared passion for the outdoors led them to establish Extreme Honeymoons, allowing other couples to experience the thrill of adventure.

Their honeymoon was an unforgettable mix of wild mountain biking near Moab and a thrilling 3-day white water rafting trip on the Colorado River. Over time, they embraced extreme sports such as parasailing and ice climbing, believing that a shared sense of adventure is the key to a strong and lasting marriage.

Where We are Today...

Extreme Honeymoons has been in business since 1976, and over 2000 couples have enjoyed these high-octane adventures. Living in Utah allows the Russons to leverage the state's vast array of outdoor activities to cater to even the boldest honeymooners.

Honeymoon Adventures

Looking for a next-level honeymoon experience? Extreme Honeymoons offers a variety of heart-pounding adventures that can be customized to your comfort level and interests. Some of our most popular tours include:

  • Ice Climbing on Bridal Veil Falls: 1-2 day Tour
  • Bungee Jumping at Dead Horse Point: 2-3 day Tour
  • Bow Hunting or Pursuing Cougars: 1 week Tour
  • Heliskiing on Kings Peak: 1-2 day Tour
  • 4WD Safari Jeep Rendezvous in Moab: 1 week Tour
  • Swimming with the Carp at Utah Lake: 1-2 day Tour

Whether you crave snow-packed slopes, rugged mountain trails, or breathtaking river rapids, our honeymoon adventures are designed to provide a memorable start to your married life.
