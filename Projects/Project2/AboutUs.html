In the Beginning...

Ted and Betty Russon have been happily married for 30 years. They fondly recall their adventurous honeymoon at Arches National Monument.

The Russons have continued to embrace adventure by regularly participating in extreme sports together, keeping the excitement alive in their marriage.

Their own honeymoon was unforgettable, involving wild mountain biking near Moab, Utah, through the beautiful red rocks and a thrilling 3-day white water rafting trip on the Colorado River.

Where We are Today...

Extreme Honeymoons has been successfully creating unforgettable honeymoon adventures since 1976. Over 2000 couples have enjoyed these high-octane experiences.

Living in Utah allows the Russons to leverage the state's vast range of adventure activities.

Popular Adventure Tours:

  • Ice Climbing on Bridal Veil Falls: 1-2 day Tour
  • Bungee Jumping off of Dead Horse Point: 2-3 day Tour
  • Bow Hunting or Pursuing Cougars in the Manti-Lasal National Forest: 1 week Tour
  • Parasailing from the Top of Mount Timpanogos: 1-2 day Tour
  • 4WD Safari Jeep Rendezvous in Moab: 1 week Tour
  • Swimming with the Carp in Utah Lake: 1-2 day Tour
  • Heliskiing on Kings Peak: 1-2 day Tour
  • High Speed Drag Racing on the Salt Flats: 3 day Tour
  • Bobsled and Ski Jumping at Utah Olympic Park: 2-3 day Tour

What Makes Us Unique

Extreme Honeymoons removes all stress by organizing every detail of your adventure. Equipment, transportation, meals, and training are all provided.

Couples can choose guided tours or opt for independent adventures. Have your own daring idea? Just share your dream with the Russons, and they will make it happen.
